Cottage Cheese Casserole

Recipe by Olena Golovchenko

Ingredients

  • 500 g cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp semolina
  • 5 dried apricots

Instructions

  1. Mix cottage cheese, eggs, chopped dried apricots and semolina.
  2. Pour the mixture into a baking dish.
  3. Place the baking dish in an unheated oven.
  4. Set the temperature to 180°C for baking.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes until lightly golden.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
